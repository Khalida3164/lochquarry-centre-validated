Our climbing activities will help build physical stregnth and give a whole new perspective to the world around us. Fully supervised fun

Our fully supervised water activities ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all young people aged 6-18. Our qualified staff are always on hand to provide guidance and support, allowing participants to challenge themselves and build confidence in a secure environment.

Abseiling

Discover the thrill of the vertical world. Take the scary step and abseil from the top of one of the local quarry slabs. Challenge yourself and achieve something incredible. Experience the ultimate rush. There is a lovely view... if you are brave enough to look down!

Max group size 8.

Ages 8+

Climbing

Embrace the challenge of the climb. Push your limits by defying gravity and learn to climb confidently under the watchful eye of our experienced team leaders. Scaleing the heights of one of the local quarry slabs is definatly an adrenaline rush that has to be experienced.

Max group size 8.

Ages 8+

Pole_Climbing

Ever wondered how telephone engineers get to the top of the telephone poles? Well, here’s your chance to find out and build functional strength and confidence while doing it. Getting to the top not only gives one the sense of achievment but the view from the top makes you feel on top of the world.

Max group size 8.

Ages 8+
